Mark Wilson

Retired Pastor in the United Methodist Church

I am an Ordained (Full) Elder in the Kentucky Conference of the United Methodist Church. I have over 40 years experience as Solo Pastor, Senior Pastor and Interim Pastor of various U.M. Churches. I've started a new church, taken a relocated church meeting in a school and built the first unit, and also was the senior pastor of a church that completely relocated to a new property. I retired in 2017. Since then I have served as an interim pastor in two U.M. Churches.
